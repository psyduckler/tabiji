‚ö° Before You Go ‚Äî Essentials

Getting Around

Get a Suica/PASMO IC card at the airport ‚Äî it works on all trains, buses, and even convenience stores. The Tokyo Metro + JR Yamanote Line combo covers 95% of what you need.

Weather

Mid-April averages 15‚Äì20¬∞C (59‚Äì68¬∞F) with occasional rain. Pack a light jacket and compact umbrella. Cherry blossoms may still be blooming ‚Äî especially late-blooming yaezakura varieties.

Cash vs Card

Japan is increasingly card-friendly, but many izakayas, ramen shops, and market stalls are cash-only. Withdraw yen at 7-Eleven ATMs (no fees with most international cards).

Reservations

Book Sushi Saito and Tempura Kondo months ahead via hotel concierge. teamLab Borderless sells out ‚Äî buy tickets online in advance. Shibuya Sky also needs advance booking.

Language

English is limited outside tourist areas. Download Google Translate with Japanese offline pack. Learn sumimasen (excuse me) and oishii (delicious) ‚Äî locals love it when you try.
