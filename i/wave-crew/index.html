‚ö° Before You Go ‚Äî Essentials for Groups

Rent 4x4s or ATVs

Nosara's roads are unpaved and spread out. For a group of 5+, rent two 4x4 SUVs (~$70‚Äì90/day each) or grab ATVs from Coconut Riders (~$60/day). You'll need your own wheels ‚Äî Uber doesn't exist here.

Cash is King

Many spots are cash-only (colones preferred, USD accepted at tourist spots). There's a BCR ATM in the village and one near Guiones ‚Äî withdraw early, they run out on weekends.

February = Perfect

Peak dry season. Sunny every day, 30‚Äì33¬∞C (86‚Äì91¬∞F), zero rain, warm 27¬∞C ocean water. No wetsuit needed. Best surf conditions of the year.

Book Group Activities Ahead

February is high season. Book surf lessons, horseback tours, and restaurant reservations at least a week in advance for groups of 5+. WhatsApp is how everything is booked locally.

‚òÄÔ∏è Afternoon ‚Äî Departure

Drive to Liberia Airport

Allow 3 hours for the drive back to Liberia (LIR). The road out of Nosara is unpaved for the first 30 minutes and can be slow behind trucks. Stop in the town of Nicoya for a quick casado at a local soda if you're hungry. Drop your rentals, check in, and start the group chat about next year's trip.

Leave Nosara by 10‚Äì11am for afternoon flights. The unpaved road is slower than you think, especially on weekends with more traffic. Don't cut it close.
