‚ö° Before You Go ‚Äî Essentials

üåßÔ∏è Rainy Season

Late February is peak rainy season in Madagascar. Expect afternoon downpours (mornings are often clear). Pack a light rain jacket, waterproof bag, and quick-dry clothes. Roads to Andasibe can be muddy ‚Äî a 4x4 transfer is recommended.

üí∞ Currency & Costs

The Malagasy Ariary (MGA) is the local currency. Madagascar is very affordable ‚Äî a great meal costs $5-10, taxis $2-5. ATMs exist in Tana but carry some cash. Many places are cash-only.

üöó Getting Around

Taxis are the main way to get around Antananarivo. Agree on the fare before getting in (no meters). For day trips, hire a driver through your hotel ‚Äî much safer and more reliable than self-driving.

ü¶† Health & Safety

Antimalarial medication is recommended. Drink bottled water only. Street food is delicious but choose busy stalls. Antananarivo is generally safe but watch for pickpockets in crowded markets.
