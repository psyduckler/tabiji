The Time Cost: DIY Research vs. Paying $1

Building a quality travel itinerary yourself takes 10–20 hours of research across Reddit, blogs, Google Maps, and transit sites. Paying $1 compresses that into a 2-minute order form.

Here's where those hours go when you do it yourself:
